## Notes

- **Flexibility built in:** If you miss a day, don't cascade — just hit the next one. Deload weeks (week 4 of each month) dial everything to 60% volume.
- **Energy check:** Midday (12–2pm) is your peak. Hardest training and focused music work should land there when possible.
- **Wed studio is the anchor.** Everything pivots around arriving fresh-voiced and present. Don't add other hard work that day.
- **Partner time is real:** Fri/Sat evenings are protected. This prevents burnout and keeps the relationship fed.
- **Prep matters:** Overnight oats/smoothies, pre-cut veggies, pre-planned guitar/vocal structure. Reduce friction where you can.

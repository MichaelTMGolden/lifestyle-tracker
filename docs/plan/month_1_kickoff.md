# Month 1 — Kickoff & Foundation

This month is about establishing habits and building the infrastructure for the year. It's intentionally not ambitious on results — it's about consistency.

---

## Week 1 — Setup (Do these FIRST)

### Booking & Logistics

- [ ] **Book vocal coach session** (Melissa Cross online, or local coach in your area)
  - Aim for week 2 or 3
  - Cost: Usually $60–150/session
  - What to bring: Phone with voice memos of your attempts at false-cord screams/fry (even rough recordings)

- [ ] **Verify gym + climbing gym access**
  - Know the hours
  - Have a gear checklist ready (climbing shoes, chalk, lifting gloves if you use them)
  - Identify a consistent time slot for each (e.g., climbing always 12:00–13:30)

- [ ] **Set up meal prep space**
  - Containers prepped? (Get glass meal-prep containers if you don't have them)
  - Recipes picked for Sunday? (Pick 2 proteins, 2 carbs, 2 veg combos for the first month)
  - Grocery list template created?

- [ ] **Set up songwriting capture system**
  - Dedicated Notes app folder or Google Keep folder for lyrics (`#lyric`)
  - Voice memo folder on phone for melodies (`#melody`)
  - Weekly review ritual: every Sunday, open and review captures
  - Back up to Drive or cloud weekly (don't lose ideas)

- [ ] **Print or pin this schedule**
  - Weekly timetable on your phone (screenshot)
  - Daily tracker printed (if you like paper) or set as recurring template in Notes
  - Put the monthly review dates in your calendar

- [ ] **Connect with your band**
  - Confirm studio Wed time slot is locked for the year
  - Tell them you're planning 6 new songs + vocals recorded (manage expectations on timeline)
  - Get feedback on what songs need: just vocals? Guitar rework? Arrangement help?

---

### Daily Habit Setup

- [ ] **Morning routine lock-in**
  - Set phone alarm for 7:00am (non-negotiable for 7 days)
  - Lay out what you'll do: mobility mat, guitar, water bottle, coffee setup
  - Do it the same way all 7 days (consistency > perfection)

- [ ] **Overnight oats / breakfast prep**
  - Make 3–5 days of oats or smoothie packs this Sunday
  - Store in fridge
  - Goal: eat in 5 min during morning routine or right after

- [ ] **Work calendar setup**
  - Block 8:15–12:00 every weekday as "deep work / no meetings"
  - Turn off Slack, email, notifications during these blocks
  - This is sacred

## Week 2 — Vocal Coach Session

**This is the most important thing this month.**

### Before the Session

- [ ] Write down 3 questions:
  1. "How do I transition safely from fry to false-cord screams?"
  2. "What should my daily warmup look like specifically?"
  3. "What are the red flags I should stop immediately?" (hoarseness, pain, strain)

- [ ] Record 2–3 voice memos:
  - One of your best clean singing (a song or scales)
  - One of your attempt at fry/low screams (raw, don't filter)
  - Send to coach before session if possible (saves time)

- [ ] Clear 90 min of distraction-free time

### During the Session

- [ ] Record it (audio or video) if they allow
- [ ] Ask them to show you in real-time what it should feel like
- [ ] Do the exercises they show you — don't just listen
- [ ] Ask for a written sheet of your new daily warmup (they often provide)

### After the Session

- [ ] Watch/listen to recording that day
- [ ] Integrate their warmup into your daily 25-min morning routine starting tomorrow
- [ ] Don't attempt anything they said "not yet" on

### Music Milestone

- [ ] Vocal coach warmup is now part of morning routine ✓
- [ ] Recorded a clean reference take (one song or scale passage)

### Work Milestone

- [ ] Upskilling slot: identify what you're learning (LLM patterns? GCP intro?)
- [ ] Shipped anything? (Not required, just noting)

## Week 4 — Deload + First Review

This is the lighter week. Intentional.

### Training Deload

- [ ] Climbing: 60% intensity — technique day only, no projecting
- [ ] Lifting: 60% volume — same exercises, fewer sets/weight
- [ ] Cardio: 60% — easy walks instead of Zone 2 effort
- [ ] Vocals: clean only, no harsh, gentle warmup
- [ ] Reason: recovery feeds next quarter. Don't skip this.

### Songwriting Deload

- [ ] No new song starts
- [ ] Review + refine what you have from Song 1
- [ ] Listen to reference tracks (artists you love)
- [ ] Journal on emotions/themes for upcoming songs

### Monthly Review

- [ ] Complete the tracker: "Monthly Check-In" section
- [ ] Honest assessment: what stuck? What felt forced?
- [ ] One thing to keep, one thing to adjust for Month 2

### Questions to answer:

1. Did the 25-min daily vocal warmup feel sustainable? ☐ Yes ☐ Somewhat ☐ No
   - If no: what got in the way?

2. Did the 25-min daily guitar feel doable? ☐ Yes ☐ Somewhat ☐ No
   - If no: scale down to 15 min or move timing?

3. Is the songwriting block time working? ☐ Yes ☐ Somewhat ☐ No
   - If no: should it be earlier, later, or different length?

4. Is partner time being protected? ☐ Yes ☐ Mostly ☐ No
   - Critical: if this is slipping, other things are bleeding in. Fix it.

5. Sleep consistency: ☐ 7+ nights by 22:30 ☐ 4–6 nights ☐ <4 nights
   - If struggling: push work start earlier? Remove screen time before bed?

6. Energy/burnout (1–10): __
   - Expected: 5–7 (building new habits is effortful)
   - If >8: ease up on something
   - If <4: schedule is too tight, revise

## What Likely Breaks Month 1 (and how to fix it)

### Scenario 1: "I missed morning routine on days 3, 4, 5"
- **Don't spiral.** Just restart day 6.
- **Debug:** Was it the time? (Try 7:15 wake)? The location? (Move guitar to different room)? Sleep? (Earlier bedtime)?
- **Fix:** Make ONE tiny change for week 2. Try it.

### Scenario 2: "Songwriting feels empty/I have no ideas"
- **This is normal.** Songwriting doesn't wait for inspiration; it IS the practice.
- **Fix:** Do a 10-min journal session before songwriting block. Write about your week, frustration, anything. The emotions prime ideas.
- Also: listen to 3 songs by artists you love before writing. Input → output.

### Scenario 3: "My voice feels hoarse after harsh vocals practice"
- **Stop harsh immediately.** Go clean-only for 2 weeks.
- **You might be rushing.** This is why the coach session is month 1 — work with them on it.
- **Red flags:** Pain (not fatigue), persistent hoarseness, inability to hit notes you normally can. Mention it in coach session.

### Scenario 4: "Work is creeping into training time"
- **Expected.** Startup life.
- **Fix:** Protect 8:15–12:00 ruthlessly. If work bleeds past 12:00, you're in trouble. Move it earlier or reschedule training.
- **Boundary:** Tell your team: "Deep work block 8–12, I respond after." Stick to it.

### Scenario 5: "I'm exhausted and partner is frustrated"
- **You're doing too much, or recovery isn't real.**
- **Fix:** Pick ONE thing to drop for 2 weeks. Not the plan, just scale it. Climbing→2x/week. Songwriting→2 blocks instead of 4. Give yourself space.
- **Partner check:** Fri/Sat is sacred. If it's not happening, the whole thing breaks. Rebalance immediately.

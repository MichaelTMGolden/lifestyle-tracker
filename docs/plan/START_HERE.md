   - This is how you last all year

## Common Mistakes (Don't Do These)

1. **Trying to be perfect from day 1**
   - Aim for 80% consistency. That's winning.
   - Perfection is unsustainable and breeds burnout.

2. **Not protecting the deep work block**
   - This is when your career happens. Guard it.
   - Meetings, messages, and Slack kill momentum.
   - 8:15–12:00 is yours.

3. **Skipping the vocal coach session**
   - Self-taught harsh vocals is expensive (months of recovery from injury).
   - Book it. Go. It's the ROI play of the year.

4. **Adding complexity too fast**
   - Month 1 is about rhythm, not results.
   - Don't add new training styles, new songs, new techniques.
   - Lock the routine first.

5. **Neglecting recovery**
   - Deload weeks feel like "wasting time."
   - They're not. Recovery is where adaptation happens.
   - Skip deload, you plateau or regress.

6. **Partner time gets eaten**
   - This starts small ("just one evening").
   - Six weeks later, you're alone and wondering why.
   - Protect Friday/Saturday nights like your life depends on it.

7. **Songwriting becomes "someday"**
   - It's easy to defer because there's no deadline.
   - Treat the songwriting blocks like gym sessions — you go no matter what.
   - Songs get written in blocks, not in "free time."

8. **Comparing your month 1 to someone's month 6**
   - You're building foundations. It takes time.
   - Month 1 goal: consistency. Month 2 goal: quality. Month 3+: performance.
   - Trust the progression.

## How to Adapt (You Will Need To)

**Adjustments happen at the monthly review.** Not daily. Not weekly. Monthly.

### If Something Isn't Working:

1. **Identify it** (in weekly review or daily notes)
2. **Let it go for a week** (one data point doesn't mean change)
3. **Bring it up in monthly review**
4. **Pick ONE thing to adjust**
5. **Try it for a month**

### What You Can Adjust:

- **Timing:** Move training to different time of day? Move songwriting earlier?
- **Intensity:** Climbing 2x/week instead of 3x? Songwriting 3 blocks instead of 4?
- **Content:** Different upskilling focus? Different climbing grade focus?

### What You Can't Adjust:

- **Morning routine 7:00–8:00** — this is the anchor
- **Deep work 8:15–12:00** — this is work
- **Partner time Fri/Sat** — this is your relationship
- **Weekly review Sunday** — this is how you track
- **Deload week 4** — this is recovery

## Quick Reference — What to Do If...

**I missed Monday's songwriting block:**
- Add 45 min to another day this week. Thursday or Saturday works.

**I'm sore/fatigued from climbing/lifting:**
- Scale training to 60% volume that day (lighter weight/easier problems). Don't skip.

**I feel flat or uninspired for songwriting:**
- Do the warmup, listen to reference tracks (artists you're studying), journal 10 min on the emotion/topic first, then write. Often the resistance passes.

**My voice is hoarse:**
- No harsh vocals that day or next. Stick to clean only. Hydrate + rest.

**I missed the vocal coach appointment (month 1):**
- Book immediately. This is the highest-leverage item for your voice health.

**Partner is upset about schedule:**
- Fri/Sat evening time is non-negotiable protected. If you're not delivering that, the schedule has already failed. Prioritize it.

**I didn't hit my work hours:**
- Deep work in morning is the foundation. If it's slipping, something else is bleeding into it. Audit: comms, phone, distractions. Fix by next day.

**Deload week (week 4) is coming up:**
- Dial training to 60% volume. Keep vocal + guitar warmups daily but skip harsh vocals. Songwriting continues but at a lighter pace. Work stays normal.
